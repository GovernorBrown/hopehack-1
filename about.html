About US

ThinkHub is an organization that stands for education equality. We were founded to bring awareness to and fight against education inequality. We acknowledge that education is a pathway to progress through which young people acquire the skills, knowledge, and experiences to obtain good jobs and prosperous futures.

Our Mission

Combat the education inequality in America.We strive to ensure that every child is prepared to learn and succeed in the classroom by providing resources to students and parents most in need.
